## Risk Management

### Risques techniques
**🔴 Risk : Bugs critiques post-launch**
- **Mitigation** : Beta testing exhaustif, monitoring temps réel
- **Plan B** : Hotfix deployment <2h, communication transparente

**🔴 Risk : Surcharge serveurs**  
- **Mitigation** : Load testing, auto-scaling infrastructure
- **Plan B** : Upgrade infrastructure emergency, queue system

### Risques marketing
**🔴 Risk : CAC inflation rapide**
- **Mitigation** : Multi-channel approach, organic content focus  
- **Plan B** : Pause paid ads, double-down community building

**🔴 Risk : Concurrence réaction (Trainerize localisation)**
- **Mitigation** : Différenciation française forte, customer lock-in
- **Plan B** : Accélération features, partnership exclusive

### Risques business
**🔴 Risk : Product-market fit insuffisant**  
- **Mitigation** : User interviews continues, feature iteration rapide
- **Plan B** : Pivot segments (studios vs indépendants)

**🔴 Risk : Team overload**
- **Mitigation** : Priorisation ruthless, external resources
- **Plan B** : Freelancers marketing, customer success externe
